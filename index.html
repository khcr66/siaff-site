Our story

The festival that started
in a classroom.

In 2018, filmmaker Thomas Yee founded the AGOAC Filmmaking Group in Markham, Ontario — a weekly classroom teaching older adults the complete craft of filmmaking. In 2024, that community turned its annual review into a public festival: SIAFF.

Read the full story →
2018 AGOAC Filmmaking Group founded by Thomas Yee. Weekly sessions begin.
2018 – Present Weekly classes, hands-on production, and annual group critique sessions.
2023 Stop IT! — a short film by the AGOAC Filmmaking Group premiered.
2024 SIAFF launches — first festival held, with jury panel and awards.
2025–26 Annual festival grows. Community expands. Identity sharpens.
2027 Opening to the world — senior filmmakers everywhere welcome to submit.
